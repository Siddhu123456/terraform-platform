# Decision Records

## Purpose

This directory contains all Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) for the AI-Native Terraform Platform.

Architecture Decision Records document significant architectural decisions that affect the design, structure, governance, or long-term evolution of the platform.

---

## Objectives

This directory exists to:

* Preserve architectural history.
* Record decision rationale.
* Improve engineering traceability.
* Support future maintenance.
* Prevent repeated architectural discussions.

---

## Decision Record Format

Every Architecture Decision Record shall follow the template defined in:

* `docs/architecture/15_DECISION_LOG.md`

---

## Naming Convention

Decision records shall follow the naming convention:

```text
ADR-001-short-title.md
ADR-002-short-title.md
ADR-003-short-title.md
```

Decision identifiers are permanent and shall never be reused.

---

## Lifecycle

Each decision follows the lifecycle below.

```text
Proposal

↓

Review

↓

Approval

↓

Implementation

↓

Verification

↓

Archive (if superseded)
```

---

## Repository Policy

Decision records are permanent repository artifacts.

Approved decisions shall never be deleted. Superseded decisions remain available for historical reference.
